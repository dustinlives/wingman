• Device Data: Device type, iOS version, for technical support

2. How We Use Your Data

• Screenshots: Sent to Anthropic for AI analysis to generate coaching messages. Never stored by Wingman.

• Messages: Stored in your account history for your reference

• Subscription Data: Used to enforce free tier limits and manage billing

• Analytics: Aggregated metrics to improve the app (not personally identifying)

3. Data Retention

• Account data: Retained until you delete your account

• Screenshots: Not stored by Wingman (sent directly to Anthropic)

• Conversation history: Retained indefinitely for your account

• Stripe: Processes payments and billing (Stripe Privacy)

• Firebase: Stores account data and usage logs (Firebase Privacy)

5. Your Rights

• You can request your data at support@wingman.app

• You control what screenshots you share

6. Security

Your account is protected by Firebase Authentication. All communication is encrypted via HTTPS.
