## Marketing Strategy Briefing

I'm launching **Wingman**, an AI-powered dating coach app, and I'm doing all marketing through Instagram. I need your help planning the marketing materials and content strategy.

### About Wingman

**Product:** A progressive web app (PWA) that helps users craft confident, authentic dating messages using AI.

**How It Works:**
1. User selects conversation stage (6 stages: Interest → Close)
2. Describes what they want to say in the conversation
3. Optionally uploads a screenshot of the conversation
4. Claude AI generates 4 response options + strategy tips
5. User picks best option and copies it to clipboard

**Key Features:**
- Works on any phone (iOS/Android) — installable like a native app
- No app store needed (just visit the web link)
- Works offline (service worker cached)
- Google & email sign-up
- Free tier: 5 messages/day
- Paid tier: $19/month unlimited

**Conversation Stages:**
1. **Interest** — Stand out, spark curiosity
2. **Attraction** — Build emotional connection, show personality
3. **Rapport** — Find common ground, deepen connection
4. **Desire** — Create anticipation, suggest meeting
5. **Momentum** — Keep energy forward, solidify plans
6. **Close** — Finalize logistics, confirm the date

**Target Audience:**
- Primary: Single men (18-35) using dating apps (Hinge, Bumble, Tinder)
- Secondary: Anyone wanting to improve their dating messaging
- Pain Point: Overthinking messages, fear of saying wrong thing, inconsistent results
- Value Prop: Confident messages that reflect who they really are

### Current State

**Live URL:** https://wingman-pwa.web.app

**What's Working:**
- ✅ Full product built & deployed
- ✅ Payments working (Stripe integration)
- ✅ Error logging & analytics active
- ✅ All legal/compliance docs in-app (Privacy, Terms)
- ✅ Mobile-optimized UI
- ✅ 6 conversation stages fully functional
- ✅ Claude AI message generation working

**Tech Stack (for context):**
- Frontend: Vanilla JS + Firebase
- Backend: Cloud Functions + Firestore
- AI: Claude API (Anthropic)
- Payments: Stripe (live mode)
- Analytics: Firebase Analytics

### Marketing Goals

1. **Awareness:** Get people to know Wingman exists
2. **Trial:** Get them to sign up & try free tier
3. **Conversion:** Convert free users to $19/month paid subscribers
4. **Retention:** Keep them using it, build habit

### What I Need From You

Please help me plan:

1. **Instagram Content Pillars** — What types of content should I post?
   - Examples: success stories, tips, before/after, behind-the-scenes, educational, etc.

2. **Content Ideas (20-30 posts)** — Specific Instagram post concepts:
   - Hook/copy ideas
   - Hashtag suggestions
   - Best times to post
   - Content format (Reels, Carousel, Stories, static)

3. **Lead Magnet / Opt-in** — How to get emails/followers?
   - Free guide concept
   - Pre-launch tease ideas
   - Link strategy

4. **Messaging Framework** — How to talk about Wingman on Instagram:
   - Headlines that resonate
   - Pain points to highlight
   - Benefits to emphasize
   - Call-to-action language

5. **Visuals Brief** — Visual style guidance:
   - Color palette
   - Photo/screenshot styles
   - Design direction
   - Brand personality/tone

6. **Growth Tactics** — How to accelerate followers?
   - Engagement strategies
   - Collaboration ideas
   - Paid ads angles (if budget allows)
   - Community building ideas

7. **Success Metrics** — How to measure if marketing is working:
   - KPIs to track
   - Benchmarks for success
   - What to A/B test

### Important Context

- **Marketing Budget:** Limited (mostly organic/bootstrapped)
- **Time Investment:** I'll handle content creation, need strategic direction
- **Constraints:** Can't do heavy paid ads initially (validating market first)
- **Audience:** Dating app users — young, casual, value authenticity
- **Competitor Landscape:** Few direct competitors (mostly dating coach courses/content)
- **Differentiation:** AI-powered, instant, affordable, private (no coaching call needed)
